About

Built with curiosity, taste, and a little spite.

I make software that feels thoughtful on the surface and solid underneath.

I'm Chris, the person behind Maevin Labs. I build digital products because I like turning vague ideas, messy sketches, and overcomplicated problems into something clean enough to use and sharp enough to keep.

I did not come into this with some polished founder origin story and a rehearsed line about disruption. I came into it by making things, fixing what broke, learning in public, and getting just stubborn enough to keep going when the elegant version did not show up on the first try.

So yes, I am still early in this. That is part of the point. I am building taste, range, and conviction in real time, which means I care a lot about getting better, making things that hold up, and not hiding behind polished nonsense while I do it.

That has shaped how I work now: clear interfaces, strong engineering, less fluff, more taste, and a bias toward shipping something real instead of talking about it for six months like it is a hostage negotiation.
